How We Work

We operate in two-week sprints. Each sprint runs Monday to Friday over two weeks, with a kickoff, mid-sprint check-in, stakeholder review, and a close. Requests submitted via the portal feed into our backlog and are scoped and picked up in the next available sprint.

Mon · Week 1
Sprint Kickoff
Scope tickets, assign owners, align on sprint goal
Thu · Week 1
Mid-Sprint Check-in
Surface blockers, adjust scope if needed
Wed · Week 2
Stakeholder Review
Demo progress to stakeholders, collect feedback
Fri · Week 2
Sprint Close
Retro, close tickets, prep backlog for next sprint
Request Intake

Submit a request via the portal. The team reviews, scopes, and schedules it into the next sprint. You’ll hear back within 2 business days.

Typical Sprint Scope

6–10 tickets per sprint across active projects. Urgent requests can be escalated to the current sprint with manager approval.

What We Do

Increase Efficiency

Reduce manual handoffs, repetitive review steps, and spreadsheet-based work through governed automation.

Improve Productivity

Give teams faster ways to run models, inspect results, track performance, and focus on judgment-heavy work.

Build Responsibly

Keep humans in the loop, document assumptions, manage access, and preserve auditability for sensitive workflows.
